Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are the registration fees? ▼
The registration fee depends on the tournament mode:
  • Solo Battle Royale: ₹15
  • Squad Battle Royale: ₹100
  • Clash Squad: ₹200
How many players are allowed in a team? ▼
For all team battles (Squad Battle Royale and Clash Squad), a team must consist of 4 main players. You are allowed to include one substitute player.
How do we communicate with the organizers? ▼
You can communicate with the organizers via:
  • Instagram: Send us a direct message on our official Instagram page.
